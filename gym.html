About Us

Fitgainz is an independent and registered fitness centre that is affiliated to the Ghana Boxing Authority and staffed with professional instructors . The aim of Fitgainz is to engage young people and adults into physical fitness, health and nutrition, drugs and alcohol awareness. As well as health and fitness, we are working with various community partners and organisations and providing a wide range of free services including health walks,health screenings, cooking workshops and mentoring support.

Exercise and well-being should be enjoyed by everyone without any financial barriers and because of this, we have opened affordable fitness centres that will help people become healthier all over the country.

Our Vision is to become a beacon of hope to the fitness industry in Ghana & West Africa .this seal means you can run, curl, lift, squat, press, walk and gym.

Calisthenics

Calisthenics are exercises that don't rely on anything/equipment but a person's own body weight. These exercises are performed with differing levels of intensity and rhythm. Sometimes these exercises are done with light handheld tools like rings and wands.
